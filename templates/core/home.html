A core part of The Law

Pro bono cases

Justice shouldn’t depend on what you can pay. We’re building The Law so verified professionals can take on pro bono matters when a case fits—giving people who can’t afford private fees a real path to representation, not just information.

  • ✓ Professionals choose when they can offer pro bono work alongside paid matters.
  • ✓ Matching and case tools help you move from chat to a structured matter when someone steps in for free.
  • ✓ We’re MVP today—pro bono capacity grows as more advocates join the platform.

Availability varies by professional and matter type; we never guarantee a specific outcome.
