Feature 8 — Anonymous reporting

Report a rights violation

We aim to add a way to submit anonymous reports that feed a national picture of patterns. The form is not live in this MVP; you can still begin safely through guided chat on this site.

Coming soon

The full pipeline will include tokenised identity (so we never need your name to accept a report), aggregation for maps and research partners, and safeguards against abuse. That requires backend and policy work beyond this MVP.

What you can do today

  • 1 Use anonymous chat to describe what happened; the assistant is designed for sensitive topics.
  • 2 Create an account when you are ready and use case tracking to keep evidence and deadlines in one place.
  • 3 Request connection to a verified lawyer or advocate when you want hands-on help.
