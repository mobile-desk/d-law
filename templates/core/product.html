TheLaw · Product brief

Justice for every Nigerian

The MVP is this website: guided chat, the professional directory, and case tracking in your browser. We are not shipping a native mobile app—use this site on your phone the same as on desktop.

Why TheLaw exists

Millions of Nigerians face rights violations—by police, landlords, employers, and others in authority—without clear recourse. The law exists, but it stays invisible unless you can afford a lawyer or already know where to look. TheLaw closes that gap with instant guidance you can use under pressure and verified professionals when you need representation. Over time we may add deeper reporting and other tools; the current focus is a solid web experience for everyone.

  • 1 Empower citizens with accurate, plain-language knowledge of Nigerian law—available through this site whenever you are online, starting with English on the web MVP.
  • 2 Connect people with active matters to verified, integrity-rated lawyers—prioritising pro bono and affordable legal aid.
  • 3 Build a national picture of rights violations through anonymous reporting—supporting reform and safer communities.

Features and status

What the web MVP includes today, and what we may add after the core product is stable. (We are not building a separate native app in this phase.)

  • Know your rights (AI guidance)
    Web

    Describe your situation; get Nigeria-aware guidance on police encounters, tenancy, work, domestic safety, bail, and more.

  • Lawyer directory and matching
    Web

    Verified professionals, case-type fit, pro bono where capacity allows.

  • Case tracking
    Web

    Log incidents, deadlines, and messages in one private workspace.

  • Emergency SOS and record encounters
    Planned

    One-tap audio/video with secure backup is not in the MVP; it would need device-level permissions beyond a normal website.

  • Offline mode
    Planned

    Offline rights content may come later via a progressive web experience or a future app—not part of the current web MVP.

  • Voice and local languages
    Planned

    Pidgin and local languages are goals for later releases after the English web flow is strong.

  • “What to say right now” quick cards
    Web

    Memorisable scripts for stop-and-search, detention, eviction, and more.

  • Anonymous incident reporting
    Soon

    Report violations without revealing identity; aggregated for accountability. Geo-tagging would come with a dedicated reporting flow, not in the first MVP.

  • Community forum and nearby alerts
    Planned

    Moderated community features and location-based alerts are not in the MVP.
